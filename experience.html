Matthew Harris is a web developer in Albuquerque, NM. Matthew decided to become a web developer after spending three years working in a law office as a paralegal and office manager, where he created solutions for the office IT. Working with IT while running the law office made Matthew realize his true passion is in IT, which lead him to enroll in the Deep Dive Coding Bootcamp where he learned full stack web development in an intense 10 week program.

​

In the Deep Dive Coding Bootcamp Matthew learned the following languages and frameworks:

​
  • PHP
  • MySQL
  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • Bootstrap
  • AngularJS
  • JavaScript

    • Network administration of separate office and guest networks

    • Maintained Windows PCs including installation of new software and troubleshooting various issues

    • Prepared various legal documents including chapter 7 bankruptcy petitions,motions, and correspondence

    • Managed projects while coordinating with clients, attorneys and courts, while meeting deadlines
  • Valet Attendant at Santa Ana Star Casino
